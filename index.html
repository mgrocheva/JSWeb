How we work

Get in touch with us to discuss your requirements. We can talk you through the process and arrange a free consultation with our team. During the initial meeting we will listen to your ideas and vision, and give some initial ideas and suggestions on how to best achieve this within your allocated budget. Following this meeting we will send you a proposal and fee quotation.

This is a vital part of the process. Before any works can begin on site, we will meet with you several times to develop the brief, producing a full drawing package and specification schedules; culminating in the construction scope of works, budget and timeline. Any required planning applications and notifications will also happen in this period. Once you are happy with the proposal and the budget, both parties sign the contract and work on site can begin.

We understand that efficient project management is key to ensuring that a build project is successful in achieving all time and budget targets; whilst rigorously maintaining our exacting levels of quality and finish to even the smallest detail.
With every final detail checked off and perfected, the completed project is handed over to you.
This is the moment when the final invoice is paid.
